2. Information We Collect

The information we collect varies depending on your plan.

2-1. Free Version (No Account Required)

  • Personal Information: The free version does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as names or email addresses.
  • Usage Data: All usage data including timer settings, session history, and labels is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Advertising: The free version may use third-party advertising services. These services may use cookies or device identifiers for ad personalization.

2-2. Paid Versions (Standard / Pro)

  • Account Information: Paid versions require account registration for cloud sync and license management. When signing in through an external authentication provider (such as Google), we receive your email address, display name, and profile image URL from the provider.
  • Usage Data: When cloud sync is enabled, your session history, settings, and label data are stored on our servers to synchronize across multiple devices.
  • Payment Information: Web payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. (USA). We do not directly collect or store credit card numbers or payment details. See Stripe's Privacy Policy. App store purchases are governed by Google Play / Microsoft Store terms.

2-3. Automatically Collected Information

  • Server Logs: When you access our servers, information such as IP address, browser type, and access timestamp may be automatically recorded. This data is used for service stability and security purposes.
  • Cookies & Local Storage: See Section 6 for details.

3. Data Storage

  • Free Version: All data is stored in your device's local storage. We do not access this data.
  • Paid Versions: When cloud sync is enabled, usage data is encrypted and stored on Supabase (USA) servers. If cloud sync is disabled, data is stored only on your device, same as the free version.

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Because there is no universally accepted standard for responding to DNT signals, we do not currently respond to them automatically. However, you can decline optional tracking cookies via the cookie consent banner.
