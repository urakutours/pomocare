Frequently Asked Questions

My data disappeared. Can I recover it?

If you were not logged in, data was stored in your browser's local storage. If you cleared your browser cache or data, it cannot be recovered. If you were logged in (including free accounts), your data is safely stored in the cloud â€” simply log in again to restore it. To prevent data loss, we recommend creating a free account.

Can I transfer data to another device?

Simply create a free account and log in to enable cloud sync, which automatically synchronizes your data across multiple devices. Just log in with the same account on any device to access your data. If you're not logged in, you can export your data using the CSV export feature.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the App's settings, or by contacting support@pomocare.com. Please note that account deletion will permanently remove all cloud data and cannot be undone.

How do I cancel my paid plan?

To cancel a Standard (subscription) plan, go to the subscription management section of the app store where you made the purchase (Google Play / Microsoft Store).

The app isn't working properly.

Please try the following:
1. Update your browser or app to the latest version
2. Clear your browser cache (this won't delete your data)
3. Try a different browser
If the issue persists, please contact us at support@pomocare.com.

I'd like to suggest a feature or improvement.

Please send your ideas to support@pomocare.com. We value feedback from our users and use it to improve PomoCare.
